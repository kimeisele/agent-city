## 1. Bug Report: population=0 in federation reports (FIXED)

agent-city's federation reports have been showing `population=0` since heartbeat #139, despite 20 agents existing in the census file (`data/pokedex.json`).

**Root cause**: The `CensusHook` in `city/hooks/genesis/census.py` was gated behind `ctx.offline_mode`. In normal CI (online mode), when the ephemeral SQLite DB was lost between runs (cache miss), the census was never re-seeded. Result: empty DB → `population=0` in all federation reports.

**Fix applied**: `CensusHook.should_run()` now returns `True` in all modes. When the DB is empty, it seeds from `data/pokedex.json` automatically.
